Ice Cappuccino

  • Ice Cappuccino

  • Iced cappuccino is a refreshing, iced version of hot cappuccino, but instead of steaming the milk, it's placed in a plastic cup. Milk and ice are added, then espresso and milk foam are added. Another method involves placing milk and ice in a shaker, mixing them well, pouring the mixture into a cup, and then adding the espresso, creating a separate layer.
