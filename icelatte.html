Ice Latte

  • Ice Latte

  • An iced latte is a version of a regular latte, but with the difference that it's served in a plastic cup. In addition to ice and milk (filling 3/4 of the cup), espresso is added, and flavorings like vanilla or caramel can be added. An iced latte is one of the simplest types of iced coffee.
