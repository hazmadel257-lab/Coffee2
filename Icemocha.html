Ice Mocha

  • Ice Mocha

  • Iced mocha is a type of iced coffee made with milk, espresso, ice, and chocolate sauce. It's similar to a hot mocha and is a favorite drink for those who enjoy the taste of chocolate with their coffee. To prepare it, place ice in a plastic cup, fill it three-quarters full with milk, mix hot espresso with chocolate sauce, and then add this mixture to the milk and ice.
